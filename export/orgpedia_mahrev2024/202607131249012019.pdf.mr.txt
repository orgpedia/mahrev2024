Order :
Whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide his letter under reference No.1 has submitted a proposal for Re-
diversion / Change in land use of 4.03 ha. Forest land for production and storage buildings of
# Page 2
Defence Explosive products within 87.97 ha of already diverted forest land for expansion of
explosive manufacturing unit at Village-Chakdhoh, Taluka-Katol, Dist. Nagpur in the State of
Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government vide under letter reference No.2 has sent the
proposal for approval of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India, New Delhi;
AND whereas, the Government of India, vide letter under reference No.3, has raised query regarding difference in calculated area of Shape of File / KML File of Forest land and proposed
forest land.
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.4 has submitted compliance of queries
to the Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government, vide letter under reference No.5 has submitted
compliance of queries to the Government of India;
AND whereas, the Government of India after due consideration, vide letter under
reference No.6, accorded ‘In-principle’ approval to Re-divert / Change in land use of the 4.03 ha.
forest Land (revised to 3.82 ha.) for the above project under section 2 Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980. The said approval is subject to fulfillment of certain conditions
by the User Agency;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur, vide letter under reference No.7 has submitted compliance of those
conditions to the Government of Maharashtra;
AND whereas, the State Government, vide letter under reference No.8 has submitted
compliance of those conditions to the Government of India;
AND whereas, Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India,
New Delhi, vide under reference no. 9 accorded ‘Final approval’ to Re-divert / Change in land use
of the 4.03 ha. forest Land (revised to 3.82 ha.) for production and storage buildings of Defense
Explosive products within 87.97 ha of already diverted forest land for expansion of explosive
manufacturing unit in favour of M/s Solar Industries India Limited at Village - Chakdhoh, Taluka-
Katol,Dist. Nagpur in the Maharashtra State, under section 2 of Van (Sanrakshan Evam
Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980;
AND whereas, the Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests & Nodal Officer,
Maharashtra State, Nagpur vide letter under reference No.10 has requested State Government to
issue diversion order in the matter;
NOW, therefore, Government of Maharashtra hereby directs that the area of 3.82 ha.
consisting of Reserve Forest & Protected Forest Land more specifically described in the proposal
submitted vide letter under reference No. | and as per details given in the Schedule given below, be Re-diverted / Change in land use to the User Agency, 1.e. M/s. Solar Industries India Limited,
Nagpur for production and storage buildings of Defense Explosive products within 87.97 ha of
already diverted forest land for expansion of explosive manufacturing unit, at Village-Chakdhoh,
Taluka-Katol, Dist. Nagpur in the State of Maharashtra.
# Page 3
The re-diversion / Change in land use shall be effected subject to the terms and conditions
mentioned in the letter dated 14.02.2026 and dated 23.04.2026 of the Ministry of Environment,
Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi. This approval for
diversion of forest land is also subject to compliance of following conditions by the User Agency-
1. Deputy Conservator of Forest, Nagpur should ensure that fulfillment of all mentioned in the letter dated 23.04.2026 of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi, before handing over the forest land to the
user agency.
2. User Agency shall comply with all the conditions stipulated in the ‘in-principle’ and ‘final
approval’ letters of Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of
India (FC Division), New Delhi, referred above. The Conservator of Forests (T), Nagpur
shall ensure the strict compliance of the conditions by the User Agency and shall furnish
the report in this regard to Additional Principal Chief Conservator of Forests and Nodal
Officer, Maharashtra State, Nagpur from time to time.
3. For ensuring the compliances and for monitoring the duly prescribed activities and also to
carry out other statutory duties, user agency shall facilitate unhindered access/entry in the area to the local forest Officer. In case the user agency prohibits the free access or create hindrances, the permission given under this Order for the diversion of forest area for the
non-forest use shall be withdrawn.
4. Non-compliance of any of the conditions mentioned in Ministry of Environment, Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi letters dated
14.02.2026 and dated 23.04.2026, in this order and Rules & Guidelines made / issued
under Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980, shall amount to violation of
Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan) Adhiniyam, 1980 and action would be taken
accordingly.
5. In case of non-compliance of any of the above conditions, the Conservator of Forests (T),
Nagpur shall report through the State Government to the Ministry of Environment,
Forests & Climate Change, Government of India (FC Division), New Delhi as per procedure laid down in guidelines under the Van (Sanrakshan Evam Samvardhan)
Adhiniyam, 1980.
