the order
Whereas Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar is Tatka. Mandal Officer, Panchgaon, Ta. Umred, G. Nagpur, while holding the office of Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur, in exercise of the powers conferred by clause (2) (a) of rule 4 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, as it is clear that he has not taken any action in respect of illegal mining of gunpowder during that period and has violated section 3 of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Conduct) Rules, 1979, as evidenced by the fact that he has not performed the duty of his office properly, the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur, Shri Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar (No. G.O.), M.N.P. Area, Nagpur City, Collectorate, Nagpur under reference No. 1 here. He was suspended from government service by order of the 90.12.2025.
2. Whereas, General Administration Department, Government Decision No. As per the provision in the 22.04.2025, the Suspension Review Committee has reviewed the suspension of Mr. Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar and recommended their reinstatement in the government service, accordingly, the government has decided to terminate the suspension of Mr. Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar and reinstate them in the government service.
3. Therefore, now in exercise of the power conferred by Rule 4 (5) (a) of the Maharashtra Civil Services (Discipline and Appeal) Rules, 1979, by this order, Shri Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar is being reinstated in the Government service subject to pending departmental inquiry against him. After re-establishment, the post of Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar is being created in the vacant post of Naib Tehsildar, Sub-Divisional Officer Office, Moda, District Nagpur.
The ongoing departmental inquiry proceedings against Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar will continue even after his reinstatement. Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar should join immediately at the place of posting. The suspension of Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar shall expire on the date on which he enters office physically at the place of posting.
5. Mr. Adesh Vinayak Bageshwar, Naib Tehsildar, on joining the above post, shall submit his report to the Government by the Divisional Commissioner, Nagpur Division, Nagpur.
