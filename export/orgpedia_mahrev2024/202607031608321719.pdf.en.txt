Preamble: - The distribution of powers and functions between the Collector and the Additional Collector has been fixed under various Government Decisions and Government Circulars of the Revenue and Forest Department.
A report has been submitted by the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik Division, Nashik regarding the revised and harmonized allocation of these subjects after reviewing the changes in administrative procedures over time. As directed by the Chief Minister, it was directed to constitute six subject-wise Study Committees under the chairmanship of each Divisional Commissioner to suggest reforms in the functioning and structure of the administration with a view to making the administration more people-oriented, transparent and efficient. Accordingly, under the Revenue and Forest Department, No. 6 These committees were constituted by the decision of the Government here. Accordingly, in the report submitted to the Government by the Divisional Commissioner, Nashik Division, Nashik, in Annexure-19 and Annexure-20, certain changes and amendments have been suggested in the distribution of powers and functions between the Collector and the Additional Collector as well as the Resident Deputy Collector and the Deputy Collector (Revenue).

There are. Considering the current administrative needs, increased operational complexity and responsibilities, it is proposed to re-allocate the issues of the Collector and the Additional Collector.
It has come. Also, as the clear allocation of functions between the Resident Deputy Collector and the Deputy Collector (Revenue) is yet to be determined by a government decision, the district collector's offices in the six revenue divisions of the state are finding variations in the allocation of subjects. In order to ensure uniformity, uniformity and clear accountability in administration, the government was considering a clear division of subjects between the Collector and the Additional Collector as well as the Resident Deputy Collector and the Deputy Collector (Revenue).
Government Decision: - Between the Collector and the Additional Collector of the State as well as the Resident
The functions between the Deputy Collector and the Deputy Collector (Revenue) are being redistributed as follows.
