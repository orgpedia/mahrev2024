The Proposal: -
dated April 8, 2025. Pursuant to the decision taken in the Cabinet of Ministers, vide the Government Resolution dated 15th May, 2025, the "Special Abhyas Scheme-2025" has been implemented for conditional regularization and foreholdings of residential and commercial lands of Sindhi migrants (excluding Ulhasnagar, District Thane). The scheme is receiving a large response from Sindhi migrants, with several cases under process. Therefore, since additional time is required to be provided to the beneficiaries to avail the benefits of the scheme, the Government is taking the following decisions: -
# Page 2
Government Decision No: Land-2024 / Q. No.52 / J-8
The Government's Decisions:
1. The "Special Abhyas Yojana-2025" scheme implemented under the Government Decision dated 15th May, 2025 is being extended by one more year with effect from 15th May, 2026.
The letter of government dated 19th September, 2025 will be applicable to the scheme.
3. All other terms and conditions of the Government decision in question shall remain the same.
4. The said Government Decision, d. dated April 8, 2025. Revenue is being released under the powers conferred on the Forest Department, as decided in the Cabinet meetings.
The decision was taken by the Government of Maharashtra.
