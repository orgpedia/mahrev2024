Circular -
An officer of the Indian Forest Service is given additional charge of the vacant post as required from the government level as well as from the office of the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Chief of Forest Force) Maharashtra State, Nagpur, as an administrative urgency. Accordingly, the government receives proposals to approve the salary for the additional charge of the officer. While sanctioning the salary for this additional charge, action is required to be taken as per the Maharashtra Civil Services (Pay) Rules, 1981 as well as the guidelines issued by the Finance Department and General Administration Department from time to time through Government Decisions.
2. However, it has been noticed regarding the proposal received from the Office of the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Chief of Forest Force) Maharashtra State, Nagpur, that some of the proposals regarding sanction of additional charge pay are for holding additional charge for a period not exceeding two years. The Government decision here provides as follows: - (a) In the case of continuation of additional charge beyond two years, prior approval of the General Administration Department and the Finance Department is required before the expiry of the period of two years.
Proposals for post-facto approval will not be considered if the provision for additional charge continues for more than two years without prior approval.
d) The Administrative Department shall take disciplinary action against the concerned officers who continue to hold additional charge for more than two years without complying with the above provisions and sanction special pay for the same.
3. In view of the above provisions, the receipt of a proposal from the Office of the Principal Chief Conservator of Forests (Chief of the Forest Force), Maharashtra State, Nagpur, for sanction of salary for additional charge of not more than two years at the Government level is not consistent with the Government decision in question. The proposal is also being submitted to the government at the government level quarterly.
